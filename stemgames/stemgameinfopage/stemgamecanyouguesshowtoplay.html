How  To  Play  !

Can you Guess is a  STEM  variation  of  the  word  game  called   Wordle !

Enter any 4 letter animal, bird, insect, sea animals, reptile say CRAB, BEAR, FROG, DUCK.

The  game  gives  you  feedback   in  3  colours.       All  3 feedbacks are crucial to make the next guess.

Letter is  wrong 
and its position   is also  wrong

Letter is correct but its position
is  also wrong

Letter and its position both
are correct

Use  the  feedback  wisely  to  guess the correct word in 5 chances and Win the Game.
